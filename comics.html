Dear Dear

Dear Dear is a collection of 4 zines that follow a
young adult named Cass while she tours colleges
alone in Boston and learns new things about herself
and her anxiety. Cass must make a choice on what
she wants to do for the rest of her life and the pressure
of that greatly weighs on her, but she's not alone. All
4 issues are available in PDF format! This is the only
comic out as of right now, but there is more to come
in the future!
